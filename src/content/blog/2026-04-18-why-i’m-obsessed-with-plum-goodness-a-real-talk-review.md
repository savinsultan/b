I still remember my first order back in 2014. I was just hunting for a vegan brand that wouldn't kill my bank account, and I found this Indian brand with the cute purple packaging. Flash forward to 2026, and my bathroom shelf is basically a Plum showroom.What I love isn't just the products; it’s that they actually have a heart. They were the first ones to tell me they’d *never* make a fairness cream because they think everyone is prettiest in their own skin. That kind of honesty is rare, and it made me a "Plumster" for life.

**1. The Green Tea Pore Cleansing Face Wash**

If you have oily skin like mine, you know the struggle. Most face washes either do nothing or leave your face feeling like a dry desert.

The Plum Green Tea Pore Cleansing Face Wash is my holy grail. It’s a clear gel with these tiny green beads that scrub away the day's grime. Every time I use it, my skin feels "squeaky clean" but still soft. Plus, that fresh green tea smell is the best wake-up call in the morning.

Why it works: It has Glycolic Acid, which keeps my pores clear and actually calmed my breakouts in about a week.

Price: Usually around ₹199 for 50ml.

**2. The 15% Vitamin C Glow Boost Serum**

I used to be terrified of serums—they seemed so "technical." But then I tried the Plum 15% Vitamin C Serum with Mandarin.

First off, it doesn't have that weird metallic smell most Vitamin C serums have; it smells like fresh oranges. It sinks in instantly and doesn't feel heavy. After using it for two weeks every morning, my skin looked way brighter. It’s like a little bottle of sunshine that actually fades those annoying dark spots from old pimples.

Quick Tip: If you're new to this, maybe mix a drop with your moisturizer at first—15% is pretty strong!

Price: Grab it for about ₹599.



**3. BodyLovin' Vanilla Vibes Body Oil**

Okay, let’s talk about the smell. If you want to smell like a warm vanilla cupcake or a walking bakery, you need the Plum BodyLovin' Vanilla Vibes Body Oil.

I usually hate oils because they feel sticky, but this one is super light. It makes your skin look all "glazed and glowing" without ruining your clothes. I love slathering this on right after a shower while my skin is still damp—it locks in the moisture and the scent actually lasts.

My Hack: Layer it over the Vanilla Vibes lotion if you want to smell delicious all day long.

Price: It's a steal at around ₹164–₹299 depending on the size.



**The Final Verdict**

Plum isn't perfect—some of their scents can be a bit strong if you have a sensitive nose. But for a brand that is 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and actually works, I’m not switching anytime soon. They make skincare feel less like a chore and more like a little "chemistry with heart" moment in my day.
